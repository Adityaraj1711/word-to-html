Question 1:

When two dice are rolled together, the probability of getting an even number on one dice and a multiple of 3 on the other is-

Option A:

1/6

Option B:

7/36

Option C:

4/9

Option D:

5/36

Correct Option:

B

Solution

Total number ofoutcomes = 36

Total number of favorable outcomes = {(2,2), (3,2), (2,6), (6,2), (4,3), (3,4), (6,6)}

Thus, P (even number on one and multiple of 3 on another) = 7/36
